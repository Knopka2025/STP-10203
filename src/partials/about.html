About the Game

What Is Paradise Paws?

Paradise Paws: Merge Animals is a relaxing and magical merge puzzle game where players bring life back to an abandoned sanctuary. Combine items, rescue animals, and design flourishing ecosystems filled with charm and discovery.

  • Requirements: Android 6.0+
  • Installs: 50,000+ players worldwide
  • Genre: Merge Puzzle Adventure
  • Updated: July 2025
